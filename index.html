Hi, my name is Charlotte Pouw and I am a PhD student at the Institute for Logic, Language and Computation (ILLC) at the University of Amsterdam. As part of the NWO project "InDeep: Interpreting Deep Learning Models of Text and Sound" (NWA.1292.19.399), I work on interpreting speech recognition models using cognitively and linguistically inspired approaches. My supervisors are Jelle Zuidema (University of Amsterdam) and Afra Alishahi (Tilburg University).
